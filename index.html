    Before I could speak, I could draw. I hold a B.A. in Studio Art from Florida State University, where I was bred to be proficient in classical & digital media.

    • Dry media (graphite, chalk, pastel, etc)

  • Philosphy of Vision

    I'm best known for pioneering the "Neutralizing" color-mapping algorithm, which I cover over at Medium.com (80,000 reads). This contribution has been featured on the front page of Reddit, as well as Fast Company.

    I'm concerned with the nature of perception. In particular, how perceptual experience relates to beliefs about reality. I hold a B.S. in Biological Science from Florida State University with specific interests in Bio-Physics, Optics, & Colour Vision.
